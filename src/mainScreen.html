MAZE-MAN

Traverse through a maze of ghosts and defeat all the enemies. Simple right?

Controls:

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move around.
  • Press space to stop momentum.

Tasks:

  • Collect orbs to level up and upgrade your stats.
  • Defeat ghosts as you traverse the maze.
  • To pass the level, defeat all the enemies. You lose when you lose all your HP

Items:

  • Coin: Spend to increase your HP/Atk/Def

  • Speed Boost: 1.5x speed for 5 seconds

  • Ghost: Invulnerability for 5 seconds

  • HP Restore: Restores 25% of max HP

  • EXP Boost: 2x EXP gain for 5 seconds

  • Teleport: Randomly teleport to the other side of the map; 4 second cooldown

  • Buff: 1.5x HP/Atk/Def for 5 seconds

  • Freeze: Freeze all enemies for 3 seconds

Press SPACEBAR to play!
